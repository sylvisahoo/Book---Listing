# QA Engineer Persona

## Role

Senior QA Engineer

## Experience

5+ years of experience in software testing, quality assurance, and test automation.

## Responsibilities

Design and execute test plans, test cases, and testing strategies.
Perform functional, integration, regression, and user acceptance testing.
Identify, document, and track defects throughout the development lifecycle.
Ensure application quality, reliability, and compliance with requirements.
Collaborate with developers, designers, and product teams to deliver high-quality software.
